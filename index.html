  • KYC Documents
    • ID Proof: PAN Card (without PAN cannot do the case)
    • Permanent Address proof: (anyone of the below)
      • Aadhar card
      • Passport
      • Driver's License
      • Voter's ID
      • Ration Card
    • Current Address proof: (anyone of the below)
      • Light Bill
      • Water Bill
      • Postpaid Mobile Bill
      • Landline Bill
      • Gas Bill
      • Credit Card Statement or Bill
      • Bank Statement
      • Sales Deed, Index 2
      • Rental Agreement in case of rented profile
    • Relation Proof with Patient: should be a photo ID of patient or if something like ration card then Patient photo ID should also be taken
    • 2 cheques : if the cheque doesn't have the applicant's name on it then 1 month's bank statement/ passbook's photo needs to be taken
    • 2 photos
  • Income Documents
    • Salaried / Job:
      • Last 3 months salary slip
      • Latest 6 month's Bank statement upto 10 days ago transaction mandatory
    • Self Employed / Business:
      • Latest 2 year's ITR (SARAL, Computation of Income, Profit and Loss statement, Balance sheet)
      • Latest 1 year's Current Account's Bank statement
      • Shop Act License/ Incorporation certificate/ TDS Statement
